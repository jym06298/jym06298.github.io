How to Shoot a Soccer Ball

Why?

Shooting is one of the most important and fundamental skill you can learn in Soccer. Learning to shoot the ball with accuracy, power, and precision can enhance any players' chances of doing well in the game of Soccer. A consistent shot starts with the fundamentals, and it is nearly impossible to have a good shot with poor technique. However, with practice and the help of this website, anyone can put in the work to have a great shot.

Correct technique

In order to learn to shoot a soccer ball with power and precision, one must make precautions to use the right technique. These precautions will help with many problems that will make your shot unpredictable. The most important factors to consider when shooting is:

  • Form
    1. Lock ankles
    2. Follow through
    3. Land on swinging foot
  • Placement foot
  • Point of impact
