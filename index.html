Why?

Shooting is one of the most important and fundamental skill you can learn in Soccer. Learning to shoot the ball with accuracy, power, and precision can enhance any players' chances of doing well in the game of Soccer. A consistent shot starts with the fundamentals, and it is nearly impossible to have a good shot with poor technique. However, with practice and the help of this website, anyone can put in the work to have a great shot.

Correct technique

In order to learn to shoot a soccer ball with power and precision, one must make precautions to use the right technique. These precautions will help with many problems that will make your shot unpredictable. The most important factors to consider when shooting is:

  • Form
    1. Lock ankles
    2. Follow through
    3. Land on swinging foot
  • Placement foot
  • Point of impact
Correct technique
Factorscorrectimportance
FormAnkles locked,chest over the ball,knees slightly bentKeeps shot from being unpredictable
Follow throughDo not stop swinging leg on impact of the ballAllows power in the shot without skyrocketing
Point of impactSlightly below or above dead center of ball with the metatarsalAllows a straight trajectory of ball

First, placement of the planting foot should be around 6-12 inches from the ball. If you are right footed, then plant with your left foot and vice versa. As you are running up to the ball, make sure to keep your eyes on the ball during the entire duration of your shot. Cock back your swinging leg as far back as possible, and swing through the ball towards the target. The follow through is very important as it helps keeps the ball straight and low.
